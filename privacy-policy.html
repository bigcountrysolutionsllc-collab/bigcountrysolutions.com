Privacy Policy

Big Country Solutions LLC  ·  Last Updated: March 28, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use the BCS Fuel platform.
Section 1

Introduction

Big Country Solutions LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the BCS Fuel mobile application and related services.

By using the BCS Fuel app, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Section 2

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you register and use BCS Fuel, we collect information you provide directly, including your full name, phone number, email address, delivery address, and payment information. Payment details are processed and stored by our third-party payment processor and are not stored directly on BCS Fuel servers.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the app, we automatically collect certain information, including your device type and operating system, IP address, app usage and session data, delivery address history, and in-app activity during delivery windows for the purpose of delivery verification.

Location Information

With your permission, we collect precise location data to determine service availability in your area and to facilitate accurate delivery to your location. You may disable location access through your device settings, though this will limit the functionality of the app.

Section 3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Process and fulfill your fuel delivery orders.
  2. Verify completed deliveries and resolve disputes.
  3. Process payments and issue receipts.
  4. Communicate with you about your orders and account.
  5. Improve the app and our services.
  6. Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.
  7. Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized activity.
Section 4

Delivery Verification Data

BCS Fuel records GPS coordinates, timestamped delivery photos, geofence arrival and departure data, and customer in-app activity for every order. This data is used exclusively for delivery verification and dispute resolution purposes. It is stored securely and is not shared with third parties except as required by law or in the context of a formal dispute process.

Section 5

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  1. With independent contractor drivers only to the extent necessary to fulfill your delivery order, such as sharing your delivery address and contact number.
  2. With our payment processor to complete transactions securely.
  3. With law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by applicable law or valid legal process.
  4. In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of the assets of Big Country Solutions LLC.
Section 6

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide services and comply with our legal obligations. Delivery verification data including GPS records, photos, and activity logs is retained for a minimum of 24 months to support dispute resolution.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us directly, subject to any legal retention requirements.

Section 7

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Section 8

Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete the data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Section 9

Children's Privacy

BCS Fuel is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected information from a person under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app following notification of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
