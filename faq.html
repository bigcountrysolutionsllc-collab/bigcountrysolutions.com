Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BCS Fuel mobile fuel delivery in Connecticut, New York, and New Jersey.

What is BCS Fuel?

BCS Fuel is an on demand mobile fuel delivery service. We bring gasoline and diesel directly to your location in Connecticut, New York, and New Jersey. You order through the app and a trained driver comes to you.

How does BCS Fuel work?

Download the BCS Fuel app, create your account, add your vehicle, and place an order. A driver arrives at your location with your fuel in DOT-compliant equipment and fuels your vehicle on site. Payment is handled securely inside the app.

What areas does BCS Fuel serve?

We currently serve Connecticut, New York, and New Jersey. Enter your address in the app to confirm delivery availability in your specific area.

How much does fuel delivery cost?

BCS Fuel charges a flat $12.99 delivery fee per order. Fuel is priced at the current market rate plus a 50 cent per gallon markup. State taxes are included at checkout so you always know exactly what you are paying before you confirm.

What is the minimum order?

The minimum order is 15 gallons per delivery.

What types of fuel does BCS Fuel deliver?

We deliver regular gasoline and diesel fuel. Select your fuel type when placing your order in the app.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary based on driver availability and your location. You can track your driver in real time inside the app once your order is confirmed.

Do I need to be present during delivery?

You do not need to be present for the entire delivery. However your vehicle must be accessible at the location you provide.

Is BCS Fuel safe?

Yes. All BCS Fuel drivers use DOT-compliant portable fuel containers and follow strict safety protocols on every delivery. Every driver completes a safety check before each order.

Can businesses and fleets use BCS Fuel?

Yes. BCS Fuel serves individual customers, businesses, construction sites, fleet operators, and property managers. Contact us at support@bigcountrysolutions.com to discuss fleet fueling options for your business.

How do I pay?

Payment is processed securely inside the BCS Fuel app. We accept all major credit and debit cards.

Is there a promo code for first time customers?

Yes. Use code BCSFUEL1ST at checkout to get 50% off your delivery fee on your first order. This offer is valid through June 28, 2026.

How do I become a BCS Fuel driver?

Open the BCS Fuel app, go to your profile, and tap Become a Driver. Complete the application including your license, vehicle information, and compliance documents. Our team will review your application and contact you shortly.
